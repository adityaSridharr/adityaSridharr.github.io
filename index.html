About Me

Hey there! I'm Aditya Sridhar, an ML engineer wrapping up my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering at IIT Hyderabad. I'm deeply passionate about Machine Learning research, with particular interests in Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing.

Recently, I've been diving into Reinforcement Learning—exploring how agents can learn optimal behaviors through interaction with their environment. What really drives me is the thrill of experimentation—setting up hypotheses, designing tests, and uncovering insights from unexpected results.

I love the problem-solving aspect of coding, whether I'm working with Python, C++, Java, or SQL. I'm all about making high-performance computing solutions work in real-world scenarios.

Currently, I'm heading up AI Engineering at Valet Network, where I've built predictive models that hit 95% simulation accuracy and optimization algorithms that boosted efficiency.

Before that, I cut my teeth at Jaguar Land Rover and HeyDaw Technologies, automating workflows and fine-tuning LLMs. I'm particularly proud of my research on attention-guided CNNs for music classification and making models more robust against adversarial attacks—work that helped my team win the JLR Global Hackathon against 250+ other teams!

At the end of the day, I'm just a problem-solver who loves combining statistical theory with practical engineering to build AI systems that are not just powerful but also make sense for the real world.
